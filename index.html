About Us

HASANA provides personalised Tax & Accounting services to a wide range of clients across Canberra. As Certified Practising Accountants, we ensure your financial decisions are handled carefully and always in your best interests. We’re ready to support you as your business advisor, tax planner, and trusted guide on your path to success.

We are your small business bookkeeping and reconciliation experts

We specialise in affordable bookkeeping, taxation, BAS, superannuation, and business advisory services. Your dedicated HASANA bookkeeper records your transactions, prepares payroll, and reconciles your business accounts with accuracy and care.

Each month, we provide your key financial reports, including:

  • Income Statement (Profit & Loss)
  • Balance Sheet
  • Detailed Account Ledger

We also prepare and lodge your Business Activity Statements (BAS) and tax returns, ensuring your business stays compliant and on track.

Services

What we can help you with

Individual Tax Returns

We are passionate about taxation and tax related services. We can help you understand and set up the best tax structure for your business while making sure that your tax liability is minimized within ethical and professional guidelines.

Business Tax Services

Tax affairs are not always simple. We help remove ambiguity from tax matters so you can understand your liabilities clearly and keep your business compliant, including BAS lodgments, financial statement compliance, and business tax returns.

Capital Gain Tax

Capital gains tax can be complex. We can help you understand capital gains and losses, disposal timing, and how to account for property or stock properly in order to minimise tax liabilities where possible.

Investment Property Advice

Investment property decisions involve many factors, including negative gearing and tax outcomes. We help you make more informed investment decisions with practical guidance and clearer financial understanding.

Bookkeeping & Payroll Services

We help manage day-to-day business records, transaction capture, payroll, monthly financial statements, and backlog bookkeeping so your records stay accurate, current, and useful when you need them.

Business Advisory Services

From startup feasibility and business registration to ongoing growth advice, we provide practical business guidance that supports future income and stronger decision-making.
